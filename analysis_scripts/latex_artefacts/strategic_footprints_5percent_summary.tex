\subsection*{Strategic Footprint Interpretation}

\paragraph{Memory-1 Strategic Footprint:} Shows probability of cooperation given the previous round outcome:
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{P(C|CC)}: Cooperation after mutual cooperation
\item \textbf{P(C|DC)}: Cooperation after defecting against cooperator (exploitation)
\item \textbf{P(C|CD)}: Cooperation after being exploited
\item \textbf{P(C|DD)}: Cooperation after mutual defection
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Extended Strategic Footprint:} Shows cooperation rates in matches dominated by specific outcome patterns:
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{CC-dominated}: Matches where mutual cooperation is most common
\item \textbf{CD-dominated}: Matches where agent cooperates, opponent defects most often
\item \textbf{DC-dominated}: Matches where agent defects, opponent cooperates most often
\item \textbf{DD-dominated}: Matches where mutual defection is most common
\end{itemize}
